Account Registration and Waiting List

To create an account, you must sign up and provide the necessary details. Upon registration, your account will be placed in the waiting list, pending approval from an admin.

Once your account is approved, you will receive a notification via email, and your account will be attributed one of the following user types:

  • User: Users with this type can access both Caesar and AES encryption features, as well as the KeyVault for managing secrets.
  • Low User: Low Users can only access the Caesar Cipher encryption feature.
